# Chapter Banner Spotlight Balance

Purpose: track featured PCs so chapter banners rotate focus instead of repeating the same foreground cast.

Rules:
- Favor the chapter's core event first.
- Keep 3 to 5 featured characters per banner.
- If a chapter only shows part of the party, prefer PCs who have had less spotlight in nearby chapters.
- Over an arc, every PC should get at least one clear, readable appearance.
- NPCs can take focus when the event requires it, but not at the cost of permanently sidelining PCs.
