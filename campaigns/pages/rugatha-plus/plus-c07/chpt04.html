15051.05.11

冒險者上船後，對船隻進行了一番調查，但並未發現任何異狀。

深夜時分，喜利・歐喜利隱約聽見一個聲音低語，告訴他——有人跟著他們上船了。

於是，他獨自搜尋整艘船，最終在船艙中找到了瑪莉。瑪莉表示，既然冒險者是要去幫助米多莉，她也希望能盡一份力，因此才偷偷跟了上來。

隔天，瑪莉提醒眾人，由於娜塔莎港對正統派津菈教極為反感，他們應避免暴露與教會的關係。同時，她也提供斗篷與藥水，協助冒險者後續行動。

傍晚過後，船隻在細雨中緩緩駛入娜塔莎港。港口空無一人，整座小鎮顯得破敗而死寂。

就在眾人下船之際，一道黑影從船上竄出，迅速消失在夜色之中。

眾人面面相覷，但仍決定先前往津菈教在當地的據點——新津菈教會。該處由希娜修女負責。

冒險者刻意隱瞞來此尋找米多莉的真正目的，只表示自己是瑪莉修女的朋友。希娜修女提到，5/13晚上川納唐普爵士將舉辦一場晚宴，鎮上居民皆會出席，這讓冒險者感到十分可疑。

他們同時推測，米多莉最有可能出現的地方，就是位於小鎮另一端山丘上的唐普宅邸。詢問前往方式後得知，這座破敗小鎮幾乎只有一條主路，只需沿著港口的道路前行即可抵達。

於是，冒險者暫時借住在教堂，準備隔天一早在鎮上打聽消息，尋找潛入宅邸的機會。

隔天一早，冒險者來到鎮上，偶然幫助了一名名叫彼得的男子。他正忙著將大量物資搬上車。

在協助過程中，他們與彼得攀談，得知這些物資正是要送往唐普宅邸，用於晚宴準備。

於是，冒險者找了個理由加入他的工作，打算藉此混入宅邸。

15051.05.11

After the adventurers boarded the ship, they conducted an investigation on the ship, but found nothing unusual.

Late at night, Siri vaguely heard a voice whispering, telling him that someone was following them on the boat.

So he searched the entire ship alone and finally found Mary in the cabin. Mary said that since the adventurer was going to help Midori, she also wanted to do her part, so she followed him secretly.

The next day, Mary reminded everyone that since Port Natasha was extremely antipathetic to the orthodox Zinla sect, they should avoid revealing their ties to the church. At the same time, she also provides cloaks and potions to assist adventurers in their follow-up actions.

After dusk, the ship slowly sailed into Natasha Harbor in a drizzle. The port was deserted and the whole town looked dilapidated and lifeless.

Just as everyone was getting off the boat, a black shadow emerged from the boat and quickly disappeared into the night.

Everyone looked at each other in shock, but still decided to go to Xinjinla Church, the local stronghold of Jinla Sect first. The place is in charge of Sister Hina.

The adventurer deliberately concealed the real purpose of coming here to find Midori, and only said that he was a friend of Sister Mary. Sister Hina mentioned that Sir Trump will hold a dinner party on the evening of May 13th, and all the residents of the town will attend, which makes the adventurers feel very suspicious.

They also speculated that the most likely place for Midori was the Temple Mansion on the hill at the other end of the town. After asking how to get there, I learned that there is almost only one main road in this run-down town, and you only need to follow the road to the port to reach it.

Therefore, the adventurer temporarily stayed in the church and prepared to inquire about information in the town the next morning, looking for opportunities to sneak into the mansion.

Early the next morning, the adventurer came to the town and accidentally helped a man named Peter. He was busy loading a large amount of supplies into the car.

While assisting, they chatted with Peter and learned that these supplies were going to the Temple Mansion for dinner preparations.

Therefore, the adventurer found a reason to join his work and planned to sneak into the mansion.
