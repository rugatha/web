15048.7.19

經過了一晚的睡眠，冒險者們醒來了，但大家都感到十分疲憊，沒睡好。就像旅店的名稱「夜夢者」，每個人都做夢了。

大家紛紛來到了勞迪俱樂部吃早餐，畢竟這是他們唯一知道能用餐的地方，並試圖從布魯斯那裡打聽更多消息。

早餐後，冒險者們拜訪了鎮長沃倫家，與鎮長戴洛與鎮長夫人潔絲敏致意。他們在鎮長說明山米克的事件後（那天早上，山米克早早出門，卻被發現倒在鎮上拉索斯教堂寂靜殿的門口，再也沒醒來），在鎮長的帶領下，前往山米克的墓前簡單致意。

比起戴洛鎮長，相對冷靜的鎮長夫人潔絲敏和冒險者們談妥並簽了契約。他聽說森林另一端的魏斯科鎮上有一名資深的拉索斯教大法師，名為葛蘭倫。法力高強的他，應該有辦法幫忙「解除詛咒」。潔絲敏委託冒險者們到魏斯科鎮，將葛蘭倫帶回沃瓦倫來解除詛咒。戴洛鎮長先給了每名冒險者50枚金幣，並承諾在他們歸來後，再付給他們各150枚金幣。

冒險者們接著來到了寂靜殿，參與了神殿負責人寇頓神父主持的祈禱儀式，隨後進入了寂靜殿，與神父進行了談話，也透過殿內的年輕學徒凱認識了寂靜殿的環境。

在勞迪俱樂部用餐後，冒險者們到道金店鋪，帕拉丁、米多莉和薩梅爾買賣了點武器，嗚兮噢尼和Tai Tai我還要也請託老闆格拉罕幫忙鍛造強化自己的武器。

接著，冒險者們到了對街的巴姆的雜貨店。除了一個 Tai Tai我還要買下的神秘項鍊外，大家似乎沒有特別想買的物品。

日落前離開沃瓦倫鎮時，米多莉從遠方看見了一個人影，便跑了過去。那人是鎮長夫人潔絲敏。潔絲敏好心的準備了一籃的三明治，讓冒險者們可以在森林裡有東西可以吃，米多莉對此十分感激。然而，因為米多莉沒有解釋清楚剛剛跑走的理由，嗚兮噢尼起頭，和米多莉起了爭執。

離開城門時，守門的兩位衛兵向冒險者們詢問了名字，冒險者思索了一陣子，最終做出了決定。他們回答道：「我們是『台北市議員』」。接著，他們便步向了神秘的嚎叫森林……

15048.7.19

After a night of sleep, the adventurers woke up. But everyone felt exhausted. They didn't sleep well. Like the name of the hostel, "Night Dreamers", everyone had a dream.

Everyone came to Rowdy's Club for breakfast, since this is the only place they knew that serves food, and try to get more information from Bruce.

After breakfast, the adventurers visited the mayor's house, the Warren, and paid their respect the Mayor Daryl and his wife, Jasmine. After learning what happened to Sammick (he went out early in the morning, but was found collapsed at the front of the local Laxthos church, Temple of Silencio, and never woke up again), Daryl lead them to Sammick's grave to pay respect.

Comparing to Mayor Daryl, Jasmine, who is rather calm, made the adventurers an offer. She heard that there was an experienced Laxthos archmage, named Grenland, in the town of Wescoe, across the forest. And it seemed like the powerful Grenland would be able to lift the curse. Jasmine asked the adventurers to travel to Wescoe and bring Grenland back to lift the curse. Mayor Daryl gave the adventurers 50 gold pieces each up front, and promised to give each one of them an extra 150 gold pieces when they return.

The adventurers arrived at the Temple of Silencio afterwards, participated in the praying session held by Father Kaulton, who is in charge of the temple, and entered the temple. They talked to Father Kaulton, and learned more about the temple from the young acolyte, Kai.

After eating in Rowdy's Club, the adventurers arrived at Dawkin's Shop. Paladin, Midori and Samael bought and sold some weapons, while Usioni and Tai Tai Wo Hai Yao asked the owner, Graham, to enhance their weapon.

Then, the adventurers went to the store across the street, Bamu's Groceries. Other than a mysterious necklace that Tai Tai bought, nothing interested the adventurers.

When the adventurers were about the leave Vowalon at sunset, Midori saw someone and ran to the person. That person is the wife of the Mayor, Jasmine. The kind Jasmine prepared a basket of sandwiches, for the adventurers to enjoy in the forest. Midori was grateful for it. However, due to the fact that Midori didn't explain well why she ran awy, Usioni started up a argument against Midori.

When the adventueres went through the town gate, the two guards at the gate asked their name. The adventurers thought for some time, and made their decision. "We're 'the Tapei City Councilor'", and they walked into the mysterious Howling Forest......
