拉比帶著冒險者們，往市鎮內熱鬧的酒館走去。他們在一樓找了空桌子坐下，點了餐。

雷亞問著關於她師父和父母相關的事，其他冒險者們則更好奇關於克萊芬，這座城市的事。

放眼望去，酒館內的酒客們穿著色彩繽紛，十分快活。約有八成的客人是蜥蜴人，剩下兩成各種種族都有，但沒有龍人。而在拉比的提示下，他們注意到了三種時常出現的穿著方式：有藍色的頭帶，有紅色的上衣或外套，還有土色的褲子。依照拉比的說法，克萊芬的政治派系主要分成三個陣營。

「新書院」以藍色為主色，是知識青年、學者為主的派系，崇尚民主、知識與理想。他們認為克萊芬應該決定自己的未來，並以學者皮尼歐教授為首。

「老樹盟」則是使用紅色，由老兵、軍閥，以及大型企業為主要成員。他們認為克萊芬應回歸萊斯諾，兩者有共同的歷史與根源。而如果要達成目標，動武也是可以的選擇。利斯達上校是他們現在的領導者。

最後是土色的「市民連線」，主要是商人、公會、一般平民的組成。比起前兩派較為極端的想法，他們不想躁進，認為「維持現狀」是最安穩、合適的。要是戰爭發生，連未來都不存在，還有什麼好談的？彭薩公會長是市民連線的首領。

毛毛眼看酒館門口有一間挺大的掛衣間，好奇地走了進去。滿掛的衣物遮擋了視線，但毛毛也清楚聽見掛衣間後方角落傳來小聲的交談聲。在外頭覺得好奇的羅羅也走了進來，馬上被毛毛暗示要小聲一點。羅羅製造了推開門，腳步踏出去的幻象後，便加入了毛毛，一起偷聽。

掛衣間的角落，兩個人正在交談著。明天有場克萊芬市長的選舉辯論會。這兩人正在討論一場刺殺的計畫，刺殺的對象，是新書院的領導者，也是這次市長選舉的候選人，皮尼歐教授。聽聞此事，儘管他們尚未決定要站在哪個派系，毛毛和羅羅都認為有必要讓皮尼歐教授知情。

悄悄離開掛衣間後，冒險者們也確認拉比支持的派系是新書院。毛毛小聲地告訴大家在掛衣間聽到的內容。毛毛和羅羅也有注意到離開的兩人，一人穿著紅色的外套，另一人則穿土色的褲子。很顯然，老樹盟和市民連線有所勾結。

大家一致認同需要有個更隱蔽的環境，於是拉比幫他們和酒館詢問了樓上的客房，預訂了一間通鋪，以及一間給羅羅使用的個人客房。大家上了樓，在通鋪討論，決定前往皮尼歐教授的辦公室拜訪。拉比叫喚了他的梟，先透過牠把拜訪的消息傳達給皮尼歐教授告知，然後大家便出發了。

學校內亮晃晃的辦公室矗立，拉比敲了敲門，兩名壯碩的維安人員開了門，確認身份後便讓拉比和冒險者們進來。皮尼歐教授和他的幕僚們正在辦公室內討論著明天的辯論會內容。皮尼歐教授站起了身，和大家致意，接著便毫不保留的施放了魔法「誠實之域」。

在來回的對話中，冒險者們只知道有老樹盟和市民連線的刺殺計劃，但方法不清楚，人是誰也說不出來。在毛毛的同意下，一名幕僚輕輕地將手放在他的額頭上，讀取他的記憶，但很快地，毛毛也感受到這人試圖讀取更深的記憶，而他抵抗失敗了。幕僚竊笑了一聲。

皮尼歐教授向冒險者們提議。他可以確保身份尷尬的雷亞在克萊芬的絕對安全，也可以提供獎金給冒險者們，但他希望冒險者們幫他一個忙：贏得後天的市長選舉。冒險者們在得到皮尼歐教授的同意下，使用了辦公室深處的獨立會議室進行討論。儘管大家沒把握現在決定自己的派系為何是不是正確的選擇，最終大家都同意，接受提案沒有壞處，於是便走出會議室，接受了皮尼歐教授的提案。

皮尼歐教授遞給每個冒險者一枚白金幣，並承諾他們事成之後再給他們各一枚。另外，他也告訴毛毛他先前在掛衣間偷取的兩個東西是什麼：一個，是被破壞的竊聽器，另一個，則是有些褪色，屬於新書院的徽章。皮尼歐教授提醒毛毛，幸好這個竊聽器早在毛毛拿到之前就已經被破壞了，否則後果不堪設想。

冒險者們在皮尼歐教授的送客下，離開了辦公室，並承諾在事成之前不公開他們合作的事蹟。他們雖然擔心皮尼歐教授明天辯論會的安危，但教授認為如果自己死去了，也是天命，而他之後，也會有其他新書院的人頂替上場，抱著他的理念為克萊芬賣命。

回到酒館，雷亞和拉比決定先到樓上休息，鑰、毛毛、羅羅和瑪莉貝爾則待在樓下和店小二攀談。他們了解到店小二是支持市民連線的人，但十分低調，只繡了一個補丁在他的衣服上來表示立場。店小二和他們推薦了當地的啤酒「克萊芬啤酒」，或簡稱「克啤」。

當雷亞經過樓上的一間包廂時，她聽見了裡面傳來的談話聲，似乎是和明天辯論會有關。她停了下來，決定變身成蟑螂，爬進去偷聽。拉比則待在門口待命。雷亞進到包廂內，一整桌的大菜，卻只有兩位並肩而坐的男子：一名身穿紅色軍裝，上面別有各類徽章，顢頇的老年灰髮蜥蜴人，和一名體格較瘦，但一臉奸邪，身穿土色精緻長袍的中年蜥蜴人。仔細一聽，這兩人對明天的「刺殺計劃」十分清楚。雷亞趕緊爬出門外，和拉比溝通。按照雷亞的描述，拉比很有把握這兩人就是老樹盟的利斯達上校，以及市民連線的彭薩公會長。

拉比聽見包廂裡頭傳來腳步聲，趕緊抓起雷亞，朝著他們的房間門口走去。一回頭，他看見酒醉的利斯達上校扶著牆，準備走向樓梯下樓去。在確認利斯達上校離開視線後，拉比回到門口，雷亞則試圖再回到包廂內。基於好奇，拉比小心翼翼地推開門縫，但看不清楚，於是他將門慢慢推開。「砰。」他聽見彭薩公會長輕輕說了一聲，一聲沉重的悶響，然後拉比胸口流出了血。他用力壓住傷口，坐臥在地上往後退。彭薩公會長走了出來，用力踩在拉比的胸膛上。「藍色垃圾雜碎。」他輕聲說道，然後朝拉比臉上吐了口口水，便走下樓了。

樓下，冒險者們和店小二聊得正盡興，鑰也得知店小二的名字為胡里恩。這時，沉重的腳步聲從階梯上走下。冒險者們看見利斯達上校扶著牆壁走下樓。鑰喊著想請利斯達上校喝杯啤酒，利斯達上校走了過來，抓起桌上新上的啤酒，朝著店門口走去，邊走邊喝，喝完後便把酒杯往後丟在地上，砸碎了也不管，打著飽嗝推開門便出去了。

「砰」的聲響也傳到了一樓。冒險者們吃驚，向胡里恩詢問。他表示那是像火一樣的魔法，是某種機械裝置。依他所知，只有貴族或極高權勢的人才有那種東西，整個克萊芬，大概最多也兩個那樣的裝置而已。冒險者們好奇店小二不用上去看看嗎？如果有這個聲響，是不是有人有狀況了，也許需要救助。胡里恩則說只要老闆沒特別說，他會裝作沒事。彭薩公會長走了下來，尖銳的眼神東看西看，然後朝地上吐了口口水，走出了門外。

Ravi led the adventurers toward a lively tavern in the middle of town. They found an empty table on the first floor, sat down, and ordered food.

Leah asked about matters related to her master and her parents, while the other adventurers were more curious about Clyphen itself and the city around them.

Looking around, they saw tavern patrons dressed in bright, colorful clothes, all in high spirits. Roughly eighty percent of the customers were lizardfolk, while the remaining twenty percent were of various other races, but there were no dragonborn. With Ravi's guidance, they noticed three clothing styles that appeared again and again: blue headbands, red shirts or coats, and earth-toned trousers. According to Ravi, the politics of Clyphen were mainly divided into three factions.

The New Academy, whose primary color was blue, was a faction made up mostly of young intellectuals and scholars, devoted to democracy, knowledge, and ideals. They believed Clyphen should decide its own future, and they were led by the scholar Professor Peneo.

The Ancient Tree League used red. Its main members were veterans, warlords, and major businesses. They believed Clyphen should return to Leisnor, since the two places shared a common history and origin. And if force was necessary to achieve that goal, then force was acceptable. Colonel Lisdar was their current leader.

Last came the Citizen's Connection, marked by earth tones, made up mainly of merchants, guilds, and ordinary civilians. Compared with the more extreme positions of the first two factions, they wanted no rash action and believed that maintaining the status quo was the safest and most suitable option. If war broke out, there might be no future left to talk about. President Bonza was the leader of the Citizen's Connection.

Fur noticed a fairly large coatroom near the tavern entrance and curiously stepped inside. Rows of hanging clothes blocked his view, but he could clearly hear hushed voices coming from the back corner of the room. Roel, curious from outside, followed him in, only to be immediately signaled by Fur to stay quiet. Roel created an illusion of the door opening and footsteps walking away, then joined Fur in eavesdropping.

In the corner of the coatroom, two people were talking. There was going to be a mayoral debate in Clyphen tomorrow. These two were discussing an assassination plan, and the target was Professor Peneo, the leader of the New Academy and a candidate in the mayoral election. Hearing this, Fur and Roel both felt that regardless of which faction they might eventually support, Professor Peneo needed to be warned.

After slipping quietly out of the coatroom, the adventurers also confirmed that Ravi supported the New Academy. Fur softly told the others what he and Roel had overheard. They had also noticed the two people who left: one wore a red coat, while the other wore earth-toned trousers. Clearly, the Ancient Tree League and the Citizen's Connection were colluding.

Everyone agreed they needed a more private place to talk, so Ravi asked the tavern about the rooms upstairs and reserved one shared room as well as a private room for Roel. The group went upstairs and discussed matters in the shared room, eventually deciding to visit Professor Peneo's office. Ravi called for his owl and had it send a message ahead to inform Professor Peneo of their visit, and then they set out.

Inside the school stood a brightly lit office. Ravi knocked, and two burly security guards opened the door. After confirming their identities, they let Ravi and the adventurers inside. Professor Peneo and her aides were in the office discussing tomorrow's debate. Professor Peneo stood up, greeted them, and then without hesitation cast the spell Zone of Truth.

In the back-and-forth conversation, the adventurers could only say that there was an assassination plot involving the Ancient Tree League and the Citizen's Connection, but they did not know the method and could not identify the people involved. With Fur's consent, one of the aides gently placed a hand on his forehead and read his memory. But very quickly, Fur sensed that the man was trying to probe even deeper, and he failed to resist. The aide let out a quiet snicker.

Professor Peneo made a proposal to the adventurers. She could guarantee absolute safety for Leah, whose identity was awkward in Clyphen, and she could also provide the adventurers with money. In return, she wanted them to help her with one thing: win the mayoral election the day after tomorrow. With Professor Peneo's approval, the adventurers used a private meeting room deeper inside the office to discuss the offer. Even though none of them were certain whether choosing a faction now was the right move, they ultimately agreed that there was no downside to accepting the proposal. So they stepped out of the meeting room and accepted Professor Peneo's offer.

Professor Peneo handed each adventurer one platinum coin and promised another one after the job was done. She also told Fur what the two items he had stolen from the coatroom actually were: one was a broken bugging device, and the other was a somewhat faded badge belonging to the New Academy. Professor Peneo warned Fur that it was fortunate the bugging device had already been broken before he picked it up, or the consequences could have been disastrous.

Escorted out by Professor Peneo, the adventurers left the office and promised not to reveal their cooperation before the matter was settled. Though they were worried for Professor Peneo's safety at tomorrow's debate, she believed that if she died, then that would be fate, and that someone else from the New Academy would step forward after her and continue fighting for Clyphen in her name and under her ideals.

Back at the tavern, Leah and Ravi decided to go upstairs and rest first, while Kaname, Fur, Roel, and Maribel stayed downstairs chatting with a waiter. They learned that the waiter supported the Citizen's Connection, but in a very low-key way, showing his stance only by sewing a patch onto his clothing. The waiter recommended the local beer, "Clyphen Beer," or simply "Cly-beer."

As Leah passed one of the private rooms upstairs, she heard voices inside that seemed to be discussing tomorrow's debate. She stopped and decided to transform into a cockroach and crawl inside to listen in. Ravi stayed by the door as lookout. Inside the room was a table covered with lavish dishes, but only two men sat side by side: an elderly gray-haired lizardfolk in a red military uniform covered with various insignia, broad and heavyset, and a thinner middle-aged lizardfolk with a sly look, dressed in an elegant earth-toned robe. Listening carefully, Leah realized that these two knew the details of tomorrow's assassination plan very clearly. She hurried back out and relayed what she had heard to Ravi. Based on Leah's description, Ravi was quite certain that the two men were Colonel Lisdar of the Ancient Tree League and President Bonza of the Citizen's Connection.

Hearing footsteps from inside the room, Ravi quickly grabbed Leah and headed toward the door of their room. When he looked back, he saw the drunken Colonel Lisdar leaning against the wall, preparing to head downstairs. After confirming that Lisdar was out of sight, Ravi returned to the door while Leah tried to go back into the private room. Out of curiosity, Ravi carefully pushed the door open a crack, but could not see clearly, so he slowly pushed it wider. "Bang." He heard President Bonza say softly, and then came a heavy dull impact. Blood spilled from Ravi's chest. Pressing hard on the wound, he fell backward to the floor. President Bonza stepped out, planted a foot hard on Ravi's chest, and whispered, "Blue trash." Then he spat in Ravi's face and walked downstairs.

Downstairs, the adventurers were in the middle of an animated conversation with the waiter, and Kaname also learned that his name was Hurian. Then the sound of heavy footsteps came down the stairs. The adventurers saw Colonel Lisdar leaning against the wall as he descended. Kaname called out, offering to buy him a beer. Lisdar walked over, grabbed the newly served beer from the table, and headed toward the tavern door, drinking as he went. After finishing it, he casually threw the glass behind him, smashing it on the floor without a care, let out a belch, pushed the door open, and left.

The sound of "bang" also carried down to the first floor. The adventurers were alarmed and asked Hurian about it. He explained that it was a fire-like kind of magic, some sort of mechanical device. As far as he knew, only nobles or people of extremely high status had access to such things, and in all of Clyphen there were probably no more than two of those devices. The adventurers asked whether the waiter should not go upstairs and check. If that sound had gone off, perhaps someone was hurt and might need help. Hurian replied that unless the owner specifically told him otherwise, he would pretend nothing had happened. President Bonza then came downstairs, his sharp eyes scanning back and forth. He spat on the floor and walked out the door.
